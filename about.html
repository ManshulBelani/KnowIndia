Indian Heritage


Heritage is our legacy from the past, what we live with today, and what we pass on to future generations. Our cultural and natural heritage are both irreplaceable sources of life and inspiration. Places as unique and diverse as the Pagodas of Mahabalipuram, the Taj Mahal, the caves of Ajanta And Ellora and the Baroque cathedrals of Latin America make up our world's heritage.
What makes the concept of Indian Heritage exceptional is its national application. Indian Heritage sites belong to all the peoples of the country, irrespective of the territory on which they are located.
Our website seeks to encourage the identification, protection and preservation of cultural and natural heritage around the country considered to be of outstanding value to humanity.

Our mission mission is to:

  • encourage citizens to ensure the protection of their natural and cultural heritage
  • encourage States Parties to establish management plans and set up reporting systems on the state of conservation of their Heritage sites
  • help States Parties safeguard Heritage properties by providing technical assistance and professional training
  • provide emergency assistance for Heritage sites in immediate danger
  • support public awareness-building activities for Heritage conservation
  • encourage participation of the local population in the preservation of their cultural and natural heritage
  • encourage international cooperation in the conservation of our world's cultural and natural heritage
